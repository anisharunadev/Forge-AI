## 4. Update protocol

1. **Cadence.** The Architect PATCHes this file at the day-30, day-60, and day-90 checkpoints. The CTO co-signs in [FORA-294](/FORA/issues/FORA-294).
2. **Format.** The **Target** rows stay verbatim. The **Actual** rows are filled with concrete metrics — links to PRs, ADRs, contracts, and audit events. Vague language ("mostly done", "in progress") is rejected at review.
3. **Drift detection.** If the day-30 retro surfaces a blocker that changes the day-60 target, the Architect PATCHes the **Target** row in §2 and the **Actual** row in §1 in the same commit. The CTO co-signs.
4. **Termination.** This template is replaced by a one-page retro at day 90. The retro lives as a comment on [FORA-279](/FORA/issues/FORA-279); the template is archived under `agents/architect/30-60-90-archived-<date>.md`.
